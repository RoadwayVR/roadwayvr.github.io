SUMO Basic Tutorials

Detailed walkthroughs for the first five tutorial parts: building networks, editing geometry, using TraCI, and configuring vehicle types and network conversions.

*

Part 1: SUMO Quick Start

  • Installation and creation of a simple network.
  • Add cars and intersections (unsignalized / signalized).
  • SUMO files and naming: networks, routes, and demands.
*

Part 2: SUMO NetEdit

  • NetEdit quick start and user interface.
  • Step length, delay, and lane-changing parameters.
  • Create and edit networks directly on GIS maps.
*

Part 3: SUMO TraCI

  • Connect SUMO to Python or other tools via TraCI.
  • Change traffic signals, collect detector data, and log results.
*

Part 4: SUMO NetConvert

  • Use OSM Web Wizard to import real networks.
  • Convert OpenDRIVE and other formats into SUMO networks.
*

Part 5: Vehicle Types & Behavior

  • Configure passenger cars, bikes, scooters, and more.
  • Car-following, lane-changing, and driver behavior parameters.
*

SUMO Advanced Topics

Advanced topics from immersive technology and CAVs to reinforcement learning, emergency management, and GIS integration.

*

Part 6: SUMO & Immersive Technology

  • SUMO2Unity: from 2D SUMO to 3D Unity in minutes.
  • Setting up co-simulation for VR-based traffic safety studies.
*

Part 7: SUMO & Autonomous Vehicles

  • Fundamentals of connected and autonomous vehicles (CAVs).
  • Mixed traffic environments with normal cars and CAVs.
*

Part 8: SUMO & Machine Learning Algorithms

  • Fundamentals of reinforcement learning for traffic control.
  • Implementing Q-learning and deep Q-learning in SUMO.
  • Generating and comparing experiment results.
*

Part 9: SUMO & Emergency Management

  • Emergency vehicle priority and signal pre-emption.
  • Comparing networks with and without signal pre-emption.
*

Part 10: Energy, Emission, and Electric Vehicles

  • Energy and emission fundamentals for ICE and EV models.
  • Hands-on examples for evaluating energy and emissions in SUMO.
*

Part 11: From QGIS to SUMO

  • Prepare aerial imagery and GIS layers in QGIS.
  • Georeference and export networks for use in SUMO.
